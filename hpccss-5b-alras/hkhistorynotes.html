(Pre-WWII) Factors Affecting HK Modernization

Internal Factor

Description

Excellent geographical location

- HK near South China Sea and Western Pacific Ocean → easy access to mainland and Pacific region → became trading hub

- Deep-water harbor → large ships can anchor there

Free port policy

- UK declared HK free trading port → ships from any countries could enter → no taxes for entering // no tariffs for any imported goods

Comprehensive infrastructure

- Warehouse and piers near Victoria Harbor

- Taikoo and Whampoa dockyards → repair of ships // shipbuilding

- Transportation → KCR + Kai Tak Airport

Sufficient capital

- Sufficient capital from Chartered Bank (渣打銀行) + HK Shanghai Banking → provided loans + financing + insurances + trades + investments → capital for entrepot trade

(Post-1970s) Economic Restructuring of Hong Kong

Revival of entrepot trade

Factor

Reason

Internal

- Factories relocated to mainland → R/O → lower production cost

- HK lacked raw materials → less competitive compared to SEA / mainland

- Revival of entrepot → low tariffs + deep sea port → largest container terminal

External

- Improved relation between PRC and the west → trade through HK

- End of embargoes

- HK’s role → regional headquarters // investing through HK into mainland

Evolving into knowledge-based and high-value industry

Internal

- Shortage of labor → aging pop // compared to region

- Compulsory education + tertiary education → 8 universities by 2000 + vocational → knowledge

- Technology → octopus cards → touchless payments

- Lacked government support for manufacturing industry

External

- Protectionism → increase in tariffs + quotas for foreign goods → increased cost of HK-made products → lower competitiveness

- Rise of Asian countries → 4 little dragons (SK, TW, SG, JP)

Rise of finance and service sectors

Internal

- Professionals → level of education → attracting foreign talents

- Legal system compatible with western countries

- Infrastructure → airport (city centre) // ports // terminals // railway

- Low tax rate // monetary stability → linked // pegged with USD 7.76~7.78

- Geographical location → coastal + close to mainland (SZ + DG) // located in Asia → formed 24 hours financial service in HK → invest in different countries
